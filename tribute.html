Imad Awan

Freelance Artist

Imad is a professional concept artist and illustrator. He has worked on notable projects for Lord of the Rings LCG, Valhall, Virgin Produced, Legend of the Five Rings, Netease Games, Brotherhood by Richard Bell and many others. He also has a successful YouTube channel with 212k current subscribers. The videos he creates range from tutorials, to speed paints of his concept art. He often adds a creative spin to his videos which keeps them fun and interesting. Imad creates imaginative and ambitious concept art though illustration and photo bashing techniques.

His Skills Include:

Promotional art, concept art, digital painting, illustration, drawing, and promotional art. He is proficient in using Photoshop and ArtRage.
